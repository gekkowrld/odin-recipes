Chocolate Covered marshmallows

By whatever name you decide to call them be it mallowpuffs, vivapuffs, mallomars or even chocolate-covered marshmallow cookies, they are one of the most beloved treats.

If well kept they can stay for a long time (If a long time means around a month)!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 10 large marshmallows

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate in a microwave-safe glass or ceramic bowl in 30-seconds intervals, stirring after each interval. Do not overheat or the chocolate will scorch
  2. Dip the marshmallows in chocolate using a stick or a fork to hold them. Place on aluminum foil, and freeze. Let the marshmallows sit at room temperature for 5 minutes before serving.
  3. Enjoy your dish
